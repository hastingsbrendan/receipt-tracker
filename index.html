Rental Tracker
—

Properties & Units

Add properties and their units. Every transaction is tagged to a property and one or more units (or applies to all units).

Backup & Restore

All your data lives locally in this browser. Export a JSON backup regularly so nothing is lost if you clear browser data or switch devices.

Bank / CSV Import

Drop a CSV from your bank or credit card. The app auto-detects date, amount, and description columns and lets you bulk-categorize before importing.

AI Extraction

Receipts are analyzed by a Cloudflare Worker that extracts vendor, date, line items, and category. The Worker only sees the receipt image — nothing else from your data.

Worker: receipt-ai-proxy.hastingsbrendan.workers.dev

Danger Zone

Clear ALL local data — properties, transactions, and receipt images. Cannot be undone. Export a backup first.
